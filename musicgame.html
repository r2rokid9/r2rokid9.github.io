Art the Musician

Along with programming, research, and being an educator, I am also a musician on the side! As a classically trained pianist, I have experienced performing in front of a live audience in concert halls and in theaters. Beethoven's sonatas, Chopin's etudes and ballades, along with Liszt's Hungarian Rhapsodies are some pieces that I love performing in public. I am also quite a fan of Jarrod Radnich's virtuosic renditions!

Personal Compositions

Here are some of my own personal compositions written for piano solo. You may find more of my compositions in my SoundCloud.

Bells of the Cathedral

Arturo Caronongan · "Bells of the Cathedral"

This was one of my very first compositions. In this piece, it tells the magical story of a traveler who seeks shelter in a cathedral during a stormy weather, and he is greeted by the magical singing of bells, the chanting of monks within an assuring and benedictive atmosphere.


"UP Naming Mahal" Virtuosic Piano Solo Version"

Arturo Caronongan · "UP Naming Mahal" (Virtuosic Piano Solo version)

This is a virtuosic piano solo version of University of the Philippines' alma mater hymn entitled "UP Naming Mahal" that was heavily based on Jarrod Radnich's arrangement of "The First Noel" amongst his virtuosic arrangements.


Dreams and Melodies of Warmth "April"

Arturo Caronongan · Dreams And Melodies Of Warmth "April"

From my "Dreams and Melodies of Warmth" collection comes "April", which is the 3rd in the series and one of my favorites. It's in E Major and was actually composed in the middle of the night when I woke up with a very bad tension headache.


R2ROKID9 the Competitive Gamer

Aside from being a musician, I am quite a gamer myself! I specialize on rhythm games and I've gotten to the point where I would compete in local and international tournaments. I have the IGN (In Game Name) "R2ROKID9" for majority of the games I play.

The games I would normally compete in are Dance Dance Revolution and Jubeat. I was also the Philippine representative for Jubeat in the Bemani e-sports Championship held in 2019 and recently a 9th Konami Arcade Championship (KAC) finalist for Jubeat. I was the first KAC finalist from the Philippines since the KAC started being held annually.
